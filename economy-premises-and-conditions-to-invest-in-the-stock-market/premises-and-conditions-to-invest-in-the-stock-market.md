## When would it be the right moment to sell and avoid a wrong investment?

The recommended moment would be when the Stock Market is closing for acquiring and selling. In that moment, it is known if the investment was successful or not.

### Possible situations

+ The Stock Market closed and the action value is less than the action value, we acquired before. Our investment is not successful.

+ The Stock Market closed and the action value is greater than the action value, we acquired before. Our investment is successful.

+ The Stock Market closed and the action value is either the same or almost the same than the action value, we acquired before. Our investment is not successful nor successful.

## How can we know if the Stock Market is closing when the action value is going upwards or downwards before the Stock Market definitively closes such current day? 

If the action value was previously going upwards and the Stock Market suddenly closed where such action value decreased, then these possible situations may occur.

+ The Stock Market closed and the action value is much less than the action value, we acquired before. Our investment is not successful.

+ The Stock Market closed and the action value is much greater than the action value, we acquired before. Our investment is successful.

+ The Stock Market closed and the action value is almost the same than the action value, we acquired before. Our investment is not successful nor successful.

Our investment should be minimum, if we recently started in this kind of business. In this way, we have the chance to analyze the different situations which may eventually or suddenly happen, along the time.

## When would it be the right moment to invest?

The right moment would be when the action value, we wish to acquire, is a low value and selling such action when the action value is a higher value than before.

We could likely assume if the action value is increasing for “N” amount of days, then it would be the moment to sell such action we acquired before.

In case, “N” amount of days is a pattern where it repeats along a “X” period of time, then it would be a successful investment.

We should review if “N” amount of days and “X” period of time, have any remarkable behavior. And if it does, then such “Y” behavior could be added into the behaviors historical.

Then, we can reuse them, and choose carefully which behavior is the best fit to be applied and predict if the action value will go considerably either upwards or downwards.
