## How could a company take advantage of Deep Learning?

Deep learning could perform these automated tasks:

- Process, understand, and pick up only relevant information from large set of documents that company holds.

- Identify objects inside the office (for example: desk, light, kitchen, monitor, CPU, size-object, etc.) in order to reorganize the place of work so that all professionals feel more comfortable and be even more productive.

- Provide reports including graphs to give better understanding about any topic that company handles with high priority like for example:
	+ Company revenue: review what pro and cons points should be analyzed more carefully to increase sales next year.
