# Catching data during transmission

When we connect other devices, which are not using the same operating system. Because it might connect to other devices using Wi-Fi or other channels. This could be risky. Because, anything may happen during a transmission between Wi-Fi connectivity and data that it is transmitting by.
 
If any external Service out there, may catch that transmission between our smart device and the other device by Wi-Fi service. Then, it may change that data slightly, that we are transmitting to that device. Eventually, results are going to be different from what we are expecting.
 
So, one solution could be to know beforehand which devices we are connecting to. For example, we may connect to Smart TV and it is a device completely different and unknown. 

We need to keep in mind that we should know beforehand, we are connecting to a device which we trust on.  

Same situation occurs when we talk about people. At the beginning, we do not know what people we are dealing with. However, we pay attention and we start talking to certain people. Because, we unconsciously know that we may trust.
