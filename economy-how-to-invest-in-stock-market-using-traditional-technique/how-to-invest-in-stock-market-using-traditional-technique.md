# How to invest in Stock-Market using traditional technique?

Time ago, there was only newspaper to look for information. It was more difficult than nowadays.

In case, I want to use a traditional technique, I will try to see what is the next ***Action-Value*** after reading about Companies, Actions, and I will intend to understand the different behaviors of big Companies or small ones.

In order to know, if it is a big Company, it is because that Company were already known by a lot of people. Like, for example, Coca-Cola, Apple, and so on.

Big Company may manage large numbers, for example, about more than $100,000 USD, etc.

Normally, Companies manage less money than that.

I may read as much as possible about Companies in Stock-Market and its corresponding ***Action-Value***.

Again, to do that working about to understand information and try to foresee what ***Action-Value*** is going to be next. It is no easy. Because, sometimes, the Stock-Market’s behavior is like a Person. So, saying this last part, it is no easy to understand what it is going to happen.

However, you can see somehow that there are such similar situations, across Companies, in the Stock-Market world.
