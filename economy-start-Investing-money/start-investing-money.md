# Start Investing money

### Elemental requirements

+ Choose one company who may offer you the chance to invest small amount of money in the actions of its company.
+ Choose one or more companies to invest money, considering the first requirement mentioned. In this way, the investment and possibilities of success may increase even more.

### Detail

Taking short period of times as reading the Stock Market of the Company, you chose previously. 

You can see by yourself, the predicted action value is one of these alternatives:

+ Predicted value is the same compared to the actual action value from the day before in the stock market.
+ Predicted value is more compared to the actual action value from the day before in the stock market.
+ Predicted value is less compared to the actual action value from the day before in the stock market.

In case, any alternative may be true then you are going to do a successful investment, investing small amount of money and earning small amount of money as well.

### Conclusion

At the beginning, it can likely be bored and discouraged. However, it may be a good strategy to start investing.
